Our Mission

Dimorphic anemia is a condition characterized by the presence of two different types of anemia simultaneously, typically a combination of iron deficiency (microcytic) and vitamin B12 or folate deficiency (macrocytic) anemia. Early diagnosis of this condition is critical to ensure proper treatment and management.

Our project leverages machine learning models to predict the presence of dimorphic anemia from patient data and blood smear images. By uploading a blood smear image, the model can assist in identifying key patterns that lead to an accurate diagnosis.
